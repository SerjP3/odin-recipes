Pasta Recipe

Description

Bolognese is so much more than just a standard meat sauce. It's defined by a base of Italian sofrito (cooked down celery, carrot, and onion), a good pour of wine, and the addition of milk, resulting in an extra creamy and velvety sauce.

Ingredients:

  • 1 tbsp. extra-virgin olive oil
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 1 carrot, peeled and finely chopped
  • 1 stalk celery, finely chopped
  • 1 lb. ground beef
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1/2 c. dry white wine
  • 1 (15-oz.) can crushed tomatoes
  • 3 tbsp. tomato paste
  • 2 c. low sodium beef (or chicken) broth
  • 1 bay leaf
  • 1 c. whole milk
  • 3/4 tsp. kosher salt
  • Freshly ground black pepper

Steps:

  1. Heat pasta water
  2. Cook onions, anchovies, garlic
  3. Make the sauce
  4. Cook the spaghetti
  5. Finish the sauce
  6. Serve
